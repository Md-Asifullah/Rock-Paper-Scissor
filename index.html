How to play Rock Paper Scissor


This is a very simple game. Player may select only one option in each move out of 3 options. They are rock, paper and scissor.


Below are the simple rules for scoring:

Rock can beat scissor. Paper can beat rock. Scissor can beat paper. If both player draw the same move then it will be a draw. The first player to score 10 points wins the game.
